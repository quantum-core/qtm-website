About Quantum Method meditation
 


What is Quantum Method meditation?

Quantum is a method of meditation that reaches our deepest central awareness under conditions of stillness and simplicity. We believe that the core of our life energy is expressed along the spine at the subtle quantum interface of matter and energy. Quantum meditation brings the intellect and feelings together to commune with that quantum interface. Performing Quantum meditation will give you fast, harmonious mind-body awareness and relaxation to enable you to live a more fulfilling life.

During meditation, a person trains their mind and induces a mode of consciousness to arrive at harmony and dynamic health. Meditation is a very old discipline and has been practiced by people historically as a powerful tool for personal improvement and spiritual enlightenment.

Human beings seek pleasure and, hence, pursue sensory objects for self-gratification. What we often do not realize is that pleasure, and pain, are subjective experiences. The Quantum meditation technique works on this and helps people to find their own inner source of bliss through controlled breathing, stretching and focusing on positive sensations along the quantum interface in the spine. People who practice Quantum meditation find it works very quickly to produce feelings of happiness and relaxation they have never felt before.
 
How much does it cost to take part in Quantum Meditation?

Nothing! It’s free. We don’t believe in charging people for enlightenment and are a registered charity. We ask that people make donations if they can, and we put that money to helping others in difficult situations.

The Quantum Foundation in Bangladesh was established to help others and built a blood centre in Bangladesh, which saves lives. The Foundation also helps the deprived local community by bringing education, health and other services including safe drinking water and circumcisions. With our willingness to serve and help others, we are succeeding in changing people’s lives.
